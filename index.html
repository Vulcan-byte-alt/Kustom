Everything You Want to Know About KustomSigns Inc.

How much does a custom sign cost?

+

The cost of a custom sign depends on various factors including size, materials, complexity of the design, and installation requirements. Contact us for a personalized quote tailored to your specific needs.

What types of signs do you offer?

+

We offer a wide range of sign options including channel letters, LED signs, illuminated signs, banners, vehicle wraps, window graphics, and custom signage solutions for businesses of all sizes.

What is the typical turnaround time for a sign project?

+

The turnaround time for a sign project varies depending on the complexity and scope of the work. We strive to complete projects efficiently while maintaining the highest quality standards. We will provide you with an estimated timeline during the consultation process.

Do you offer installation services?

+

Yes, we provide professional installation services for all types of signs. Our experienced installation team ensures that your sign is installed safely and correctly, adhering to all local codes and regulations.

What is the warranty on your signs?

+

We stand behind the quality of our signs and offer a warranty against manufacturing defects. The specific terms of the warranty may vary depending on the type of sign and materials used. Ask your representative for more details!

Can you help with the design of my sign?

+

Absolutely! Our experienced design team can work with you to create a custom sign that effectively communicates your brand message and meets your specific needs. We can create from simple banner - coming soon to a great channel letter sign, any size any color and design.
